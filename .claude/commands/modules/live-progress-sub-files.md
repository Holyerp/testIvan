# Live Progress — Sub-Files

**Purpose:** Formats and update rules for the five supporting progress files (next to `DASHBOARD.md`).

**Companion module:** `live-progress-dashboard.md` (DASHBOARD.md structure + triggers)

---

## current-status.md (Detailed Status)

**Purpose:** Comprehensive status report — fuller version of DASHBOARD.md.

**Content:**
- Detailed phase breakdown
- All stories with status
- Complete blocker list
- Full quality metrics
- Budget tracking
- Timeline analysis

**Target size:** < 400 lines (comprehensive; DASHBOARD.md is the at-a-glance summary)
**Update frequency:** Daily or on-demand (generated by `/project-status`)

---

## completed.md (Append-Only Log)

**Location:** `.project-management/output/progress/completed.md`
**Target size:** Unlimited
**Update:** Append one entry per completed story; never rewrite history.

Entry format:

```markdown
## Week {{WEEK_NUMBER}} ({{DATE_RANGE}})

### US-XXX: {{story_title}} ({{points}} points)
- **Completed:** {{DATE}} {{TIME}}
- **Tests:** {{test_count}} passing
- **Coverage:** {{coverage}}%
- **Commit:** {{commit_hash}}
```

---

## blockers.md (Active Blockers)

**Location:** `.project-management/output/progress/blockers.md`
**Target size:** < 100 lines
**Update:** Manual (never auto-updated by `/execute-work` — blockers need human context).

Entry format:

```markdown
### BLK-001: Short title
**Reported:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Affects:** US-XXX, US-YYY
**Severity:** Critical | High | Medium
**Description:** What's blocking, why.
**Mitigation:** What can proceed anyway.
**Owner:** Who is unblocking.
```

Resolved blockers move to a "Resolved Blockers" section with a resolution date; never delete history.
